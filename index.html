GET INVOLVED

Thank you for your interest in getting involved in It Happens Here. We would be honored to have you as a part of our organization. Please fill out the application linked below to join. Within the application you can specify if you're interested in leadership.


Membership
Being a member will mean attending bi-weekly meetings that will continue to educate the club on the issue of sex trafficking while also discussing relevant legislation and current events. We will also be covering current and upcoming club events.


Leadership
Leadership will involve attending weekly meetings as well as the bi-weekly member meetings. Leaders would work in a leadership team to help further the organization and its mission to see human value fully recognized on our campus.

No previous involvement is required. We are looking for passionate, driven, and committed individuals who are willing to work during their own time to make a difference for this issue.
