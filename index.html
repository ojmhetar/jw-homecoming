ABOUT

Hey! I am Jordan White and I’m running for homecoming this fall. I’m a Computer Science major in the Class of 2017. You may have seen me around campus; I’m the guy with the really big blonde hair.

I’m running to raise awareness of the reality that slavery still exists in the United States. There are over 200,000 children (that’s just children) who are sold for sex in the US each year.

My campaign has partnered with Street Ransom, a Roanoke, VA based ministry that is building a safe home for under age girls who have been sexually exploited. The home will serve the entire state of Virginia. $7.00 of each campaign shirt sold is going towards the operating costs of the safe home.

I hope to raise awareness (read below how you can help in this) that sex trafficking does happen in the US and even locally in the New River Valley. I also aim to raise support for Street Ransom while shedding light on the reality of sex trafficking, prostitution, and pornography. Ultimately, I want to spread the hope of restoring true human value to the way God intended and designed it to be.

Voting for me for homecoming does not end trafficking. It doesn’t do much of anything. But, it does make a unified statement that Virginia Tech students recognize this atrocity and will work to see it ended. I ask that if you vote for me for homecoming that you would treat this vote as a pledge to use your actions and life to fight sex trafficking.
